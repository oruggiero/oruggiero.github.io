Anthropology

I hold a BA in Anthropology from Loyola University Chicago. My anthropology research interests focus on museums, art & material culture, and religion & ritual. As an undergraduate, I conducted a research project on a set of Cora festival masks (from Mexico) held in an ethnographic collection.
